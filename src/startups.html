Our Services

Pitching and Corporate Matching

We offer the opportunity to pitch your solutions to global challenges in front of expert panels; if selected, we'll match you with the most strategically aligned corporation to accelerate your market growth.

Pilot Design and Activation

We work side-by-side with you to co-design and execute pilot programs in real-world environments, using structured KPIs and hands-on support to validate impact and scalability.

Short- and Long-Term Funding Access

We secure full funding for pilot execution, and in the case of successful pilots: open doors to future funding sources through continued corporate partnerships and inclusion in active investor networks.

Strategic and Operational Advising

We provide tailored legal, financial, and strategic guidance to help you navigate regulatory frameworks, enter new markets, and manage the complexities of scaling.

Education and Professional Development

We foster connections with expert mentors and peer networks, offering training and resources to increase your internal capabilities and foster long-term growth beyond the pilot stage.

Market Access and Visibility

We amplify your startup's visibility through global innovation databases, which are delivered to top-tier corporates, investors, and ecosystem partners.
