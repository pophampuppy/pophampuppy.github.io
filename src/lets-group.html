Lets Group, the global partner for business innovation

The Lets Group, comprised of Letslaw, Letsfinance, Letsinnovate, and Letstech , is a firm specializing in legal consulting for technology businesses, startups, and corporations, as well as financial, innovation, and M&A consulting. It offers a 360-degree solution that prioritizes the unique needs of each client, both in the market and in its partners.

One of the Lets Group's main objectives is to protect the interests of individuals and companies in their operations abroad, for which they rely on an international network of partners in more than 40 countries.

Regarding its verticals, LETSLAW was founded in 2012 by José María Baños and marked the beginning of an era of innovative legal advice, specializing in commercial, digital, and technology law. The firm has established itself as a benchmark in the Spanish entrepreneurial ecosystem thanks to its commitment to excellence and innovation.

Led by Silvia Altieri, LETSFINANCE has transformed the financial advisory landscape for businesses, startups, scale-ups, and more. This branch of the group has its own unique method and approach that allows them to offer customized solutions to each of their clients, from large corporations to SMEs and technology companies.

LETSINNOVATE strengthens and boosts the way corporate innovation teams connect with startups and establishes new collaboration models that enable greater business impact. With its own approach, this branch of the group facilitates the adoption of new technologies and the development of intrapreneurship and digital transformation programs, offering customized solutions for corporations, SMEs, and technology companies.

Finally, LETSTECH was established with a 360-degree vision to provide advanced and accessible technological solutions for SMEs, enabling them to optimize their processes and expand their operations in the global market. Its expertise ranges from the creation of custom applications and digital platforms to the implementation of customized ERP systems that facilitate comprehensive operations management. Furthermore, Letstech focuses on data protection and cybersecurity, offering the implementation of cutting-edge strategies and technologies to protect SMEs' sensitive information and ensure business continuity in a secure digital environment. It also provides specialized legal support in data management and regulatory compliance.
